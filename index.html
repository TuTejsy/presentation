Blockchain - is a growing list of records, called blocks, which are linked using cryptography.

Each block contains a cryptographic hash of the previous block, a timestamp, and transaction data.

Decentralization is the Essence of Blockchain

Three main properties of Blockchain:

  • Decentralization
  • Transparency
  • Immutability

Blockchain was invented by Satoshi Nakamoto in 2008.
